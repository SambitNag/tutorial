Timing and Control Unit (TCU):

A Timing and Control Unit (TCU) is a crucial component in various systems, often used in industrial and automotive applications.

Its main functions include:

  1. Timing Control :

    2. Regulates the timing of processes or operations within a system, ensuring they occur in the correct sequence and at the right intervals.

  2. Synchronization :

    3. Ensures different parts of a system work together harmoniously by coordinating timing signals.

  3. Signal Generation :

    4. Produces timing signals or pulses required for different operations.

  4. System Monitoring :

    5. Monitors and adjusts timing parameters to maintain system efficiency and prevent malfunctions.
